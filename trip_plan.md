### ⚠️ Pork-Free Ordering Guide
You don't eat pork. Foods to actively skip:
- ❌ Samgyeopsal (pork belly BBQ), Bossam (boiled pork wraps), Jokbal (pig's feet)
- ❌ Sundae (Korean blood sausage — almost always pork)
- ❌ Jeju black pork (famous regional specialty)

### 🚨 Watch Out — Hidden Pork in "Safe-Sounding" Dishes
| Dish | Risk | What to Ask |
|------|------|-------------|
| Kimchi stew (kimchi-jjigae) | Often contains pork belly | "Pork-free kimchi stew?" |
| Tteokbokki | Broth sometimes meat-based, fish cakes may contain pork | "Vegetable broth tteokbokki?" |
| Dumplings (mandu) | Many are pork-filled | Ask for beef/chicken/kimchi/vegetable |
| Kimbap | Often contains ham unless specific variety | Order **mayak gimbap** (no meat) |
| Fried rice (bokkeumbap) | May include ham or bacon | "No ham fried rice?" |
